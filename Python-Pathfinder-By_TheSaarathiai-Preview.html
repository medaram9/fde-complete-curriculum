Python interview reality

Python interviews in 2026 combine syntax questions, data structure problems, and OOP design. The most common gaps candidates have: not understanding mutability, confusing deep vs shallow copy, and not knowing when to use a generator vs a list. Practice the *args/**kwargs and OOP scenario problems in this curriculum until you can explain them out loud.
